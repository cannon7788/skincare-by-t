-About T-

My name is Takako, but most people call me T. I am originally from Okinawa, Japan. I am a licensed esthetician in the state of California and I'm based in Oceanside in north San Diego County. I have always had a passion for making people feel good and giving them confidence in their appearance. As a military wife and working mom, I know how busy my clients are, and how precious their time and money are. I love pampering my clients and giving them a moment of peace and comfort in their busy lives. I also want to give you the best value possible for your money. I really love what I do, and I enjoy getting to know my clients. If you have any questions about the services I offer, or how I can tailor them to your needs, please feel free to ask. I am happy to discuss your skincare needs, so go ahead and drop me a line on facebook or instagram! I check them daily. I look forward to hearing from you! -T
